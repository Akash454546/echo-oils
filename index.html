BENEFITS AND USES

More information about our pure oils

Groundnut Oil

High Smoke Point, Rich in Vitamin E

  • *Use:* Ideal for deep frying, sautéing, and stir-frying due to its high smoke point.
  • *Health:* Contains unsaturated fats, promoting heart health.
  • *Flavor:* Adds a subtle, nutty flavor without overpowering food.

Sesame Oil

Antioxidant Rich, Distinct Aroma

  • *Use:* Perfect as a finishing oil, in dressings, marinades, and Asian cuisine.
  • *Health:* Rich in antioxidants and has anti-inflammatory properties.
  • *Flavor:* Deep, aromatic, and savory flavor profile.

Coconut Oil

Versatile, MCT Source

  • *Use:* Great for baking, high-heat cooking, and as a natural moisturizer for skin/hair.

  • *Flavor:* Mildly sweet and tropical when cold-pressed.

COMMON QUESTIONS

Frequently Asked Questions

What is cold-pressed oil?

Cold-pressed oil is extracted naturally using a traditional method without heat or chemical solvents. This preserves the oil's nutritional value, flavor, and aroma better than refined methods, giving you a purer product.

Are your oils suitable for deep frying?

Yes, our Groundnut Oil has a high smoke point, making it excellent for deep frying. Our Coconut Oil can also be used for high-heat cooking, while Sesame Oil is best reserved for low-heat finishing and dressings.

How should I store the oils?

For best results and to maintain freshness, store the oils in a cool, dark place away from direct sunlight and heat. Keep the bottle tightly sealed when not in use.

What is the shelf life of your oils?

Typically, our oils remain fresh for 6 to 9 months after opening, provided they are stored correctly. Please refer to the 'Best Before' date on the bottle for precise guidance.
